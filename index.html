About the Exam

What is NISM Series V-A?

The NISM Series V-A: Mutual Fund Distributors Certification Examination is mandatory for all individuals who want to distribute mutual fund products in India. Conducted by the National Institute of Securities Markets (SEBI), it is a prerequisite to obtain ARN (AMFI Registration Number).

📋

Multiple choice format, 2 hours duration

What You'll Learn

Complete NISM V-A Curriculum

Our curriculum is built directly from the official NISM workbook, structured into easy-to-follow modules.

Module 01

Investment Landscape

Financial goals, risk-return trade-off, asset classes, and the role of financial intermediaries in India.

Module 02

Concept & Role of Mutual Funds

History, structure, types of mutual funds, and the regulatory framework under SEBI & AMFI.

Module 03

Legal Structure & Constituents

Sponsors, AMCs, trustees, custodians, registrars — roles and responsibilities of each entity.

Module 04

Fund Categories & Types

Equity, debt, hybrid, solution-oriented, index funds, ETFs, and SEBI's categorisation circular.

Module 05

NAV, Pricing & Valuation

How NAV is computed, loads, cut-off timings, and portfolio valuation norms.

Module 06

Investor Services & KYC

Account opening, KYC process, transaction modes, SIP/SWP/STP, and investor rights.

Module 07

Taxation of Mutual Funds

STCG, LTCG, dividend taxation, and tax-saving ELSS funds with practical case studies.

Module 08

Risk, Return & Performance

Standard deviation, Sharpe ratio, alpha, beta, and how to evaluate fund performance.

Module 09

Financial Planning & Distribution

Goal-based planning, MFD responsibilities, ethics, ARN registration process, and compliance.
